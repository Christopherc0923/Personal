About the Website


During my spare time, I channeled my passion for web development by building this personal website using a combination of frontend and backend technologies. To enhance the website's appearance, I utilized Bootstrap, HTML, and CSS to create an interactive and visually appealing frontend. In addition, I incorporated a backend framework, Django, to ensure the website was efficient and user-friendly. To store and manage data, I used SQLite, a robust database management system. Throughout the development process, I encountered challenges, but I used my problem-solving skills to overcome them and improve the functionality and appearance of the website. I am proud of this achievement and am excited to continue enhancing my skills in web development.

Todo Application

Our to-do list application allows you to easily add new entries to your to-do list. Simply navigate to the todo section and type in the task you need to complete. Once you've added your task, you can also include a due date or any additional notes or reminders. Our user-friendly interface makes it simple to manage your tasks and stay organized, ensuring that you never forget an important task again. With our to-do list application, you can quickly and easily manage your tasks and accomplish your goals.

After adding entries to your to-do list, you can view them in the todo section of our application. From there, you can select and delete individual entries using the 'Delete' button next to each task. If you want to delete all tasks at once, you can use the 'Delete All' button at the bottom of the list. Our application makes it simple to manage and organize your to-do list, so you can focus on completing your tasks and achieving your goals.

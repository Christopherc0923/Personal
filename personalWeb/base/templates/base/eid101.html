Low Cost Diabetes Management and Diagnostic Devices for the People of Kampala, Uganda

Project Objective

The goal of the group was to tackle the growing problem of type II diabetes in Kampala, Uganda by developing a low-cost and non-invasive solution. To achieve this, the group created two non-invasive prototypes that would be used independently for diagnosis and treatment. These prototypes were designed to be affordable and accessible for individuals in Kampala. By providing an innovative and cost-effective solution to a pressing health issue, the group's work has the potential to positively impact the health and wellbeing of many people in Kampala.
